Why Choose TRASH2CASH?

Advanced TensorFlow model automatically identifies and sorts waste into proper categories with 95% accuracy, ensuring proper recycling.

Secure identification using your Pakistani CNIC. No complex passwords, just scan your QR code and start disposing responsibly.

Earn Reward Points

Get points for every disposal! Metal: 15pts, Glass: 12pts, Plastic: 10pts, Paper: 8pts. Redeem for bills, vouchers & more.

Interactive map shows smart bins near you with real-time capacity status. Never search for a disposal point again.
